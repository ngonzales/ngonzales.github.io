Corbel [kôrbəl] an architectural bracket usually used to support a structural timber. Most corbels are supported by a post or a wall.

Starting with sawn timber we produce a wide variety of corbels, cut specifically for your project.

Choose from our corbel patterns or create your own. At Wholesale Timber and Viga we cut all our corbel by the order. We can cut most orders the same day they are ordered.
